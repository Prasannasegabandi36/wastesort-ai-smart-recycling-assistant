Why WasteSort AI?

Waste sorting is simple, but people still get confused.

Many recyclable items go to landfills because users do not know whether an item belongs to dry waste, wet waste, recyclable waste, or hazardous waste.

🗑️

Wrong Bin Usage

People often mix plastic, food waste, paper, and batteries in the same bin.

⚠️

Hazardous Risk

Batteries and e-waste can harm the environment if disposed incorrectly.

📉

Low Recycling Awareness

Users need simple disposal guidance, not complicated waste rules.
